   Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

   Answer: Git is useful for version control: This allows many people to work independently on different parts of a project, without 
   conflicting. Also, it preserves historical versions so that changes do not permanently overwrite something you may need later.

   Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

   Answer: Data science can be applied to any field that deals with data, one example would be social sciences. Data science can be 
   used to analyze the responses from a wide-reaching survey to find the most effective rhetorical strategies, for example.

   Q3: What is the difference between Merging and Rebasing?

   Answer: Merging is a way of joining branches that preserves all history of both branches. Rebasing will combine branches in a cleaner
   fashion by rewriting some history but this may cause unintended side effects.
